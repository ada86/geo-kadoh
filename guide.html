Quick guide for Hi5! Geo-KadOH
Hi5! Geo-KadOH is a decentralized solution for location sharing. You can easily create an account and start sharing your location with your friends.

About log in

If you don't have an account just type any username and password, and the system will create for you an account.

Output box

This box tells you in what state the website is. It also gives you responses on whether thigs go well or not.

Locating yourself

To locate yourself and share it to your friends just push the Share my location button. Your browser may ask you to allow location sharing which is required for this functionality. Succesfully shared location will be announced in the output box and your location will shown in the map.

Finding friends

Just type your friends username and we'll see if he has shared his location. There is a list of your saved friends behind the drop down button. You can modify the list with edit button. After you have selected a friend we'll show you in the output box if the friends location was found. The friend will be then shown in the map.

Requirement

You can use both desktop and mobile devices to use Hi5! Geo-KadOH. Only requirement is that the browser or the device should support HTML5.
